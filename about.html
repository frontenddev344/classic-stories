About

We love memoirs and biographies , so we built a practice crafting them.

Inspiration

Joshua Sklare grew up surrounded by books. His grandfather wrote a memoir, America, I Love You, that inspired Josh. His father, Marshall Sklare, was a sociology professor at Brandeis University and a published author. His mother, Rose Sklare, spent her career as an editor at some of America's most respected publishers — Ginn & Co., Little Brown, Macmillan, and Harcourt Brace. She was the founding inspiration for Classic Stories.

Founding

In December 2005, a family member asked Josh to help write a private biography for family and a few close friends. After researching the field of private memoirs and biographies, he found a glaring gap. The books lacked research rigor, writing quality, and production values of the best commercially available books.

What began as a single project became a twenty-year practice. Classic Stories — originally founded as Montefiore Press, Inc. — has since produced over two dozen private volumes for some of the most accomplished men and women in America.

Mission

Classic Stories strives to entertain, enlighten, and inspire your readers while giving them an accurate and meaningful record of your journey.
